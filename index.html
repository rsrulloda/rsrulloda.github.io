My Projects

Jisho Discord Bot

Jisho is a Japanese word bot that provides a word of the day from a SQLite database. The word is displayed in English and Japanese characters, along with a definition and a part of speech. I plan on implementing more features in the future such as translation and a study mode. This bot is made using Java, Gradle, and SQLite.

PlateUp! Pretzel Mod

PlateUp! is a food service simulation game on Steam. There are a variety of base recipes that are included such as hamburgers, steak, and salad. This mod will implement additional pretzel recipes, such as almond pretzels, cinnamon nuggets, and lemonade. This mod is to be created using C# and .NET framework.

FoodWise Mobile App

FoodWise is an android app concept developed as a group for the 2023 Google Solution Challenge to help reduce food waste.The idea of the app scans a receipt and keeps track of the expiration date of the foods purchased. If the food is close to its expiration date, then the app will notify you. The app also gives you an insight to your food wasting habits. The demo version of the app was developed using Kotlin and Gradle.
